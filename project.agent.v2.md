# PROJECT MASTER PROMPT

## France Business Development OS

Build a production-ready SaaS platform called:

France Business Development OS

An AI-powered Business Development and Market Entry Platform helping international companies successfully enter the French market.

The platform must go far beyond company lookup.

The mission is:

Help users discover:

* Customers
* Distributors
* System Integrators
* Public Tenders
* Decision Makers
* Market Opportunities
* Business Partners

and generate complete France Market Entry strategies using AI Agents.

The platform should become:

"ChatGPT + PitchBook + Apollo + Tender Intelligence + Consulting Firm"

for France.
